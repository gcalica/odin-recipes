Omelette

Description

An omelette is a dish made from eggs, fried with butter or oil in a frying pan.

It is a common practice for an omelette to be folded around fillings such as chives, vegetables, mushrooms, meat, cheese, onions or some combination of the above.

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Milk
  • Butter
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Beat the eggs
  2. Melt the butter
  3. Add the eggs
  4. Fill the omelette
  5. Serve
