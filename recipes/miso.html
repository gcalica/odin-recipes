Miso Soup

Description

Miso soup is a Japanese soup made with miso paste, water, and ingredients such as dashi, tofu, and soy sauce.

Ingredients

  • Miso Paste
  • Water
  • Dashi
  • Tofu
  • Soy Sauce

Steps

  1. Mix miso paste and water
  2. Add dashi
  3. Add tofu
  4. Add soy sauce
  5. Serve
